Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting BJ's Lawn Service, Inc's website at https://www.bjslawn.com ("Website"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information when you fill out a Contact Form on our website. This includes some, or all of the following, depending on how you fill out the form:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number

We also use Google Analytics to collect data on website traffic and usage. Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle data. Additionally, we use tools by Ahrefs Services to collect data on website traffic and usage. Please refer to Ahrefs' Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle data.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect personal information through contact forms on our website. Additionally, Google Analytics automatically collects data when you visit our website.

3. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Responding to customer inquiries and new lead requests
  • Sending newsletters (if you choose to subscribe)
  • Analyzing website traffic and usage through Google Analytics
  • Analyzing website traffic and usage through Ahrefs

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share or sell your personal information with third parties.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously and use encryption. However, please note that our website does not directly handle sensitive or personal information directly.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information submitted via contact forms in our email inbox for as long as necessary to respond to inquiries or requests. We do not store copies beyond that.

7. User Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information. To exercise this right or if you have any privacy-related concerns or requests, please contact us via email or phone using the contact information below.

8. Additional Terms

This Privacy Policy solely applies to the BJ's Lawn Service, Inc. website and does not cover any external websites linked to or from our site. Furthermore, it does not cover any services provided through working with BJ's Lawn Service, Inc., as this policy only pertains to the use of the Website.
