Accessibility Policy

BJ's Lawn Service, Inc. is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including individuals with disabilities. We are continuously improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to meet legal and ethical responsibilities. We follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA to the best of our abilities, and strive for conformance in accordance with Title III of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA). This applies to our website and any digital services we may offer now or in the future.

Our Accessibility Commitments

  • Design & Development Standards - Accessibility is integrated into our website development process from the start. All updates and new content are reviewed for accessibility before publication.
  • Ongoing Audits - We perform regular automated and manual accessibility audits using a combination of internal checks and third-party tools. These audits evaluate conformance with WCAG 2.1 AA and identify improvements.
  • Remediation Process - When issues are found, we take prompt action to resolve them and minimize disruption to users. Our team maintains a formal process for tracking, reviewing, and addressing reported issues.
